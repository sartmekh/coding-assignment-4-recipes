Kiev Cutlet

Ingredients

  • Chicken fillet
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Egg
  • Milk
  • Butter (cold)
  • Sunflower oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste

Equipment

  • Meat mallet
  • Frying pan
  • Two deep plates (for egg wash and breadcrumbs)

Instructions

  1. Pound the chicken fillet until thin. Season with salt, place a piece of cold butter in the center, and fold it tightly like an envelope.
  2. Beat the egg with milk in one plate, and pour breadcrumbs into another.
  3. Dip the cutlet alternately into the egg mixture, then into the breadcrumbs. Repeat this 3-4 times to create a thick crust and shape the cutlet.
  4. Place the breaded cutlet in a frying pan with heated sunflower oil and cook over low heat until golden brown and fully cooked inside.
