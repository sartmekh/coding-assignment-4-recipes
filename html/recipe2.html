Homemade Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 9 lemons
  • 7 cups ice-cold water

Directions

  1. Combine sugar and the 1 cup water in the saucepan.
  2. Boil the water, stirring to dissolve sugar.
  3. Meanwhile, juice lemons into a measuring cup until you have 1 1/2 cups.
  4. Pour 7 cups ice-cold water into a pitcher and stir in lemon juice.
  5. Add sugar-water and ice to taste.
  6. Enjoy!
