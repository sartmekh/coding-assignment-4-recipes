Healthy Hummus

Recipe by: Wholefoodscollective.com.au

Ingredients

  • 1 tin chickpeas (approx 250g rinsed)
  • 2 tbsp tahini (hulled)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juice of)
  • 1/2 to 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin

Directions

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and cumin together.
  2. Add small amounts of water until desired thickness is reached.
  3. Serve topped with olive oil, seeds, and fresh basil.
