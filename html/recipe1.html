Waffles

Source: Classic Waffles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Heat the waffle maker.
  3. Pour batter into waffle maker.
  4. Cook until golden brown.
